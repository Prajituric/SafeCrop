How it works

Your intelligent crop protection in 6 smart steps

01

Monitor your fields in real time

On your main dashboard, you get instant access to your latest alerts, messages, live camera feed, and a visual of your field. You also receive a weekly report summarizing field activity and risks — everything in one place, always up to date.

02

Stay informed with daily agricultural insights

Check the latest news and educational content tailored to your region and crop type. SafeCrop keeps you updated on weather risks, farming tips, and best practices — helping you grow smarter every day.

03

Chat alerts with loss estimation

Every time a threat is detected, you’ll see a real-time chat alert where you can estimate how much area was affected. The app instantly calculates potential losses. From the same screen, you can jump to the GPS location or open the live camera for a better view.

04

Pinpoint the threat’s location instantly

When you tap “See where” from an alert, SafeCrop shows you the exact location on the map using GPS. From here, you can also launch the live camera view to confirm the situation visually.

05

Track your alert history and threat categories

Explore your full alert history, filtered by category: fire, pests, wild animals, weather issues, and more. Learn where most problems occur and track how your protection improves over time.

06

Analyze monthly risks and download reports

See all incidents detected in the current month — with estimated losses, risk types, and a short summary of the last 3 months. You can download your own report or explore what other farmers in the network are facing.

    Our team

    Safe Field Security

    We are Safe Field Security, a team of third-year Mechatronics students from Politehnica University of Bucharest, passionate about developing smart solutions for agriculture. Our flagship product, SafeCrop, is an AI-software with application to help farmers detect real-time threats in their fields using affordable camera systems and smart notifications.

    Our mission is to empower small and medium-sized farms by combining technology with accessibility. We believe in protecting crops and improving productivity through real-time monitoring, without relying on expensive drones or complex systems.

    We aim to tackle the growing challenges in agriculture, such as wildlife intrusions, fire hazards, pest outbreaks, and drought, which often go unnoticed until significant damage is done. By automating detection and offering instant alerts, SafeCrop minimizes human error and maximizes crop safety.

    Our vision is to build a more resilient and data-driven future for farming—one where every farmer, regardless of scale or budget, has access to smart protection tools.
